# <a name="why-a-page-is-locked-from-personalizing"></a><span data-ttu-id="e9062-103">Hvorfor er en side låst mod tilpasning</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-103">Why a Page is Locked From Personalizing</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="e9062-104">Hvis der er et låseikon i bjælken **Tilpasning**, når du åbner en side (som vist), betyder det, at du i øjeblikket er forhindret i at foretage flere tilpasningsændringer af siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-104">If there is a lock icon in the **Personalizing** bar when you open a page (as shown), this means that you are currently prevented from making any more personalization changes to the page.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e9062-105">![Tilpasning låst](media/personalization-locked.png "Tilpasning låst")</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-105">![Personalize Lock](media/personalization-locked.png "Personalize lock")</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e9062-106">Det skyldes, at vi har ændret den måde, hvorpå tilpasning fungerer i baggrunden, siden sidste gang du har tilpasset siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-106">This is because we changed the way personalization works behind the scenes since the last time that you personalized the page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e9062-107">Desværre arbejder den gamle og den nye måde at gøre tingene på ikke sammen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-107">Unfortunately, the old way and new of doing things do not work together.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="e9062-108">Siden indeholder i øjeblikket de sidste tilpasningsændringer, du har foretaget.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-108">The page currently includes the last personalization changes that you made.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e9062-109">Hvis du vil fortsætte med at tilpasse siden, skal du vælge låseikonet og derefter **Lås op**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-109">If you want to continue personalizing the page, then you can choose the lock icon and then **Unlock**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e9062-110">Du skal blot være opmærksom på, at hvis du vælger at låse siden op, fjernes den aktuelle tilpasning af siden, og du skal starte forfra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e9062-110">Just be aware that if you choose to unlock the page, the current personalization of the page will be cleared, and you will have to start from scratch.</span></span>

1.  <span data-ttu-id="e679f-130">Vælg ikonet ![Søg efter side eller rapport](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Ikonet Søg efter side eller rapport"), angiv **Købsfakturaer**, og vælg derefter det relaterede link.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e679f-130">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Incoming Documents**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>  

2.  <span data-ttu-id="e679f-131">Vælg linjen med den indgående dokumentpost, der repræsenterer en ny indgående elektronisk faktura. Under fanen **Start** i gruppen **Administrer** skal du derefter vælge **Rediger**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e679f-131">Select the line for the incoming document record that represents a new incoming electronic invoice, and then, on the **Home** tab, in the **Manage** group, choose **Edit**.</span></span>  

     <span data-ttu-id="e679f-132">I vinduet **Indgående bilagskort** tilknyttes den relaterede XML-fil, og de fleste felter er udfyldt med oplysninger fra den elektroniske faktura.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e679f-132">In the **Incoming Document Card** window, the related XML file is attached, and most of the fields are prefilled with information from the electronic invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e679f-133">Du kan finde flere oplysninger under [Oprette indgående dokumentposter](across-how-create-income-document-records.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e679f-133">For more information, see [Create Incoming Document Records](across-how-create-income-document-records.md).</span></span>  

3.  <span data-ttu-id="e679f-134">I feltet **Dataudvekslingstype** skal du vælge **PEPPOL – faktura** eller **OCR – faktura** afhængigt af kilden til det elektroniske dokument.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e679f-134">In the **Data Exchange Type** field, choose **PEPPOL - Invoice** or **OCR – Invoice** depending on the source of the electronic document.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="e679f-136">Under fanen **Handlinger** i gruppen **Generelt** skal du vælge **Opret bilag**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e679f-136">On the **Actions** tab, in the **General** group, choose **Create Document**.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="60745-104">Når du indtaster data på siderne i [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], er visse felter markeret med en rød stjerne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60745-104">When you enter data on pages in [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], certain fields are marked with a red asterisk.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60745-105">Den røde stjerne betyder, at feltet skal udfyldes for at fuldføre en bestemt proces, der bruger feltet, f.eks. bogføring af en transaktion, der bruger værdien i feltet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60745-105">The red asterisk means that the field must be filled to complete a certain process that uses the field, such as posting a transaction that uses the value in the field.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="60745-106">Selv om feltet indeholder en rød stjerne, er du ikke tvunget til at udfylde feltet, før du fortsætter til andre felter eller lukker siden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60745-106">Even though the field contains a red asterisk, you are not forced to fill in the field before you continue to other fields or close the page.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="60745-107">Den røde stjerne tjener kun som en påmindelse om, at du vil blive blokeret fra at udføre en bestemt proces.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="60745-107">The red asterisk only serves as a reminder that you will be blocked from completing a certain process.</span></span>

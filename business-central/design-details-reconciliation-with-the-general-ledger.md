<span data-ttu-id="6e672-104">Når du bogfører lagertransaktioner, f.eks. salgsleverancer, produktionsafgang eller nedreguleringer, registreres ændringen i antal og værdi for lageret i vareposterne og værdiposterne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e672-104">When you post inventory transactions, such as sales shipments, production output, or negative adjustments, the quantity and value changes to the inventory are recorded in the item ledger entries and the value entries, respectively.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="6e672-105">Det næste trin i processen er at bogføre lagerværdierne på lagerkontiene i finansregnskabet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="6e672-105">The next step in the process is to post the inventory values to the inventory accounts in the general ledger.</span></span>

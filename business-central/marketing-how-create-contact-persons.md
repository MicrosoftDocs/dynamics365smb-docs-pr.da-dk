## <a name="to-create-a-contact-card-for-a-person"></a><span data-ttu-id="02e85-109">Sådan oprettes der et kontaktkort for en person</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-109">To create a contact card for a person</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="02e85-110">Vælg ikonet ![Elpære, der åbner funktionen Fortæl mig](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Fortæl mig, hvad du vil foretage dig"), angiv **Kontakter**, og vælg derefter det relaterede link.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-110">Choose the ![Lightbulb that opens the Tell Me feature](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Tell me what you want to do") icon, enter **Contacts**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="02e85-111">Vælg handlingen **Ny**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-111">Choose the **New** action.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="02e85-112">I feltet **Nummer**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-112">In the **No.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="02e85-113">skal du skrive et nummer på kontakten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-113">field, enter a number for the contact.</span></span>

    <span data-ttu-id="02e85-114">Hvis du har defineret en nummerserie for kontakter i vinduet **Marketingopsætning**, kan du i stedet trykke på Enter og vælge det næste tilgængelige nummer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-114">Alternatively, if you have set up a number series for contacts in the **Marketing Setup** window, you can press the Enter key to select the next available contact number.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="02e85-115">Du kan finde flere oplysninger under [Oprette nummerserier](ui-create-number-series.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-115">For more information, see [Creating Number Series](ui-create-number-series.md).</span></span>
4. <span data-ttu-id="02e85-116">Vælg **Person** i feltet **Type**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-116">In the **Type** field, select **Person**.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="02e85-117">Udfyld de andre felter på kontaktkortet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-117">Fill in the other fields on the contact card.</span></span>

> [!NOTE]  
>   <span data-ttu-id="02e85-118">Indholdet af de felter, du har markeret i sektionen **Overførte oplysninger** i vinduet **Marketingopsætning**, kopieres fra virksomheden til personerne i virksomheden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="02e85-118">The contents of the fields that you have selected in the **Inheritance** section of the **Marketing Setup** window are copied from the company to the persons within that company.</span></span>

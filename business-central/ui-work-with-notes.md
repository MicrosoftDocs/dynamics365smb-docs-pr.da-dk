# <a name="add-and-view-notes-on-records"></a>Tilføje og se noter i poster
 Du <!--OnPrem and your colleagues -->kan føje noter til poster i programmet. Mange vinduer, især dem, der viser en liste over poster, f.eks. vinduet **Salgsordrer**, omfatter handlingen **Noter** eller faktaboksen **Noter**. <!--OnPrem Notes is where you can write notes about a record to yourself or others, and where you can view notes to you from others. For example, a note could be a general comment or processing instruction to your colleague, who can then respond to your note using their own **Notes**. Or, your colleague can add a note that gives you extra information about a sales order that is not covered by the information on the sales order. These notes and correspondences will follow the record as it is processed in the company.-->

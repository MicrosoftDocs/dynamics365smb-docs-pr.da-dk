## <a name="to-print-a-test-report-before-posting-a-purchase-invoice"></a><span data-ttu-id="2011c-107">Sådan udskrives en kontrolrapport før bogføringen af en købsfaktura</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2011c-107">To print a test report before posting a purchase invoice</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="2011c-108">Vælg ikonet ![Elpære, der åbner funktionen Fortæl mig](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Fortæl mig, hvad du vil foretage dig"), angiv **Købsfakturaer**, og vælg derefter det relaterede link.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2011c-108">Choose the ![Lightbulb that opens the Tell Me feature](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Tell me what you want to do") icon, enter **Purchase Invoices**, and then select the related link.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="2011c-109">Åbn den købsfaktura, som du vil teste.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2011c-109">Open the purchase invoice that you want to test.</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="2011c-110">Vælg handlingen **Testrapport**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2011c-110">Choose the **Test Report** action.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="2011c-111">Alle dele af købsfakturaen testes og resultatet vises i en rapport.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2011c-111">All parts of the purchase invoice are tested, and the result is presented in a report.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="2011c-112">Hvis rapporten angiver fejl eller manglende oplysninger, skal du løse problemerne, før købsfakturaen bogføres.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="2011c-112">If the report indicates any errors or missing information, then you must correct the problem before posting the purchase invoice.</span></span>

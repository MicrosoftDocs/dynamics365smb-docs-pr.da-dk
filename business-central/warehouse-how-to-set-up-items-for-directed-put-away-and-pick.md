## <a name="to-set-up-an-item-for-directed-put-away-and-pick"></a><span data-ttu-id="af1dc-107">Sådan opsættes varer til styret læg-på-lager og pluk</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="af1dc-107">To set up an item for directed put-away and pick</span></span>

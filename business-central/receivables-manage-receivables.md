# <a name="managing-receivables"></a><span data-ttu-id="c60e9-103">Administrere tilgodehavender</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-103">Managing Receivables</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="c60e9-104">Almindelige trin i en økonomisk cyklus er at udligne bankkonti, hvilket indebærer, at du udligner indgående betalinger til debitor- eller kreditorposter for at lukke salgsfakturaer og købskreditnotaer som betalte.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-104">A regular step in any financial rhythm is to reconcile bank accounts, which requires that you apply incoming payments to customer or vendor ledger entries to close sales invoices and purchase credit memos as paid.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c60e9-105">Mens de fleste kunder i B2B-miljøer betaler et stykke tid efter levering, og de bogførte salgsfakturaer forbliver åbne, indtil betalingen modtages, hvorefter afdelingen for tilgodehavender kan lukke (udligne) dem, kan nogle salgsfakturaer betales med det samme, for eksempel via PayPal.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-105">While most customers in B2B environments pay some time after delivery, leaving the posted sales invoices open for the Accounts Receivable department to close (apply) when payment is received, some sales invoices can be paid immediately, for example with PayPal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c60e9-106">Disse fakturaer udlignes med det samme som betalte, når de bogføres og vises derfor ikke som betalinger, der skal behandles i Tilgodehavender.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-106">Such invoices are immediately applied as paid when they are posted and, therefore, do not appear as payments to be processes in AR.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c60e9-107">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i f.eks. <span data-ttu-id="c60e9-108">I [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)] kan du hurtigt registrere betalinger på siden **Betalingsudligningskladde** ved at importere en bankkontoudtogsfil eller et -feed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-108">In [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], one of the fastest ways to register payments from the **Payment Reconciliation Journal** page by importing a bank statement file or feed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c60e9-109">Betalingerne udlignes til åbne debitor- eller kreditorposter baseret på sammenligninger af data i betalingstekst og oplysninger i poster.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-109">The payments are applied to open customer or vendor ledger entries based on data matches between payment text and entry information.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c60e9-110">Du kan gennemse og ændre de fundne data, før du bogfører kladden, og lukke bankposter for poster, når du bogfører kladden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-110">You can review and change the matches before you post the journal, and close bank account ledger entries for ledger entries when you post the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c60e9-111">Bankkontoen er udlignet, når alle betalinger er udlignet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c60e9-111">The bank account is reconciled when all payments are applied.</span></span>

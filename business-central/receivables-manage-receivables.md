# <a name="managing-receivables"></a><span data-ttu-id="287b3-103">Administrere tilgodehavender</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="287b3-103">Managing Receivables</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="287b3-108">I [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)] kan du hurtigt registrere betalinger i vinduet **Betalingsudligningskladde** ved at importere en bankkontoudtogsfil eller et -feed.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="287b3-108">In [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], one of the fastest ways to register payments from the **Payment Reconciliation Journal** window by importing a bank statement file or feed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="287b3-109">Betalingerne udlignes til åbne debitor- eller kreditorposter baseret på sammenligninger af data i betalingstekst og oplysninger i poster.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="287b3-109">The payments are applied to open customer or vendor ledger entries based on data matches between payment text and entry information.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="287b3-110">Du kan gennemse og ændre de fundne data, før du bogfører kladden, og lukke bankposter for poster, når du bogfører kladden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="287b3-110">You can review and change the matches before you post the journal, and close bank account ledger entries for ledger entries when you post the journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="287b3-111">Bankkontoen er udlignet, når alle betalinger er udlignet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="287b3-111">The bank account is reconciled when all payments are applied.</span></span>

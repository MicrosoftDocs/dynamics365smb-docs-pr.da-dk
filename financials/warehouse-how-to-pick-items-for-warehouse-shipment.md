<span data-ttu-id="704fc-130">Foruden pluk for kildedokumenter, som beskrevet i dette emne, kan du hente og placere varer mellem placeringer uden at bruge kildedokumenter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="704fc-130">In addition to picking for source documents, as described in this topic, you can take and place items between bins without referring to source documents.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="704fc-131">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Fremgangsmåde: Plukke og lægge på lager uden kildedokument](warehouse-how-to-create-put-aways-from-internal-put-aways.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="704fc-131">For more information, see [How to: Pick and Put Away Without a Source Document](warehouse-how-to-create-put-aways-from-internal-put-aways.md).</span></span>

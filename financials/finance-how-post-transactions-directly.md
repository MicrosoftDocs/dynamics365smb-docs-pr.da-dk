# <a name="how-to-post-transactions-directly-to-the-general-ledger"></a><span data-ttu-id="24fe2-103">Fremgangsmåde: Bogføre transaktioner direkte i finansposterne</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-103">How to: Post Transactions Directly to the General Ledger</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="24fe2-104">De fleste finansposteringer bogføres i finansregnskabet ved hjælp af dedikerede forretningsdokumenter, f.eks. købsfakturaer og salgsordrer.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-104">Most financial transactions are posted to the general ledger through dedicated business documents, such as purchase invoices and sales orders.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-105">Du kan oprette relaterede transaktioner for aktiviteter, der ikke er repræsenteret af et dokument i [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], f.eks. mindre udgifter eller indbetalinger, ved at bogføre kladdelinjer i vinduet **Finanskladde**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-105">For business activities that are not represented by a document in [!INCLUDE[d365fin](includes/d365fin_md.md)], such as smaller expenses or cash receipts, you can create the related transactions by posting journal lines in the **General Journal** window.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="24fe2-106">En typisk anvendelse af finanskladden er at bogføre medarbejdernes brug af egne penge under forretningsaktiviteter for senere refusion.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-106">A typical use of the general journal is to post employees' expenditure of own money during business activities, for later reimbursement.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-107">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Fremgangsmåde: Registrere og refundere medarbejdernes udgifter ](finance-how-record-reimburse-employee-expenses.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-107">For more information, see [How to: Record and Reimburse Employees' Expenses](finance-how-record-reimburse-employee-expenses.md).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="24fe2-108">Finanskladder bogfører finansposteringer direkte på finanskonti og andre konti, f.eks. bank-, debitor-, kreditor- og medarbejderkonti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-108">General journals post financial transactions directly to general ledger accounts and other accounts, such as bank, customer, vendor, and employee accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-109">Når du bogfører via en finanskonto, oprettes der altid poster i finanskonti.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-109">Posting with a general journal always creates entries on general ledger accounts.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-110">Det gælder også, når du bogfører f.eks. en kladdelinje på en debitorkonto, fordi der bogføres en post på en finanskonto for tilgodehavende via en bogføringsgruppe.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-110">This is true even when, for example, you post a journal line to a customer account, because an entry is posted to a general ledger receivables account through a posting group.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-111">Du kan tilpasse din version af en finanskladde ved at oprette et kladdenavn eller en skabelon.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-111">You can personalize your version of a general journal by setting up a journal batch or template.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-112">Du kan finde flere oplysninger under [Arbejde med finanskladder](ui-work-general-journals.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-112">For more information, see [Working with General Journals](ui-work-general-journals.md).</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="24fe2-113">I modsætning til poster, der bogføres med dokumenter, som kræver en kreditnotaproces, kan du korrekt tilbageføre poster, der bogføres i finanskladden.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-113">Unlike for entries that are posted with documents, which require a credit memo process, you can correctly reverse entries that are posted with the general journal.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-114">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Sådan tilbageføres poster](finance-how-reverse-journal-posting.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-114">For more information, see [How to: Reverse Postings](finance-how-reverse-journal-posting.md).</span></span>

## <a name="to-post-a-transaction-directly-to-a-general-ledger-account"></a><span data-ttu-id="24fe2-115">Sådan bogføres en transaktion direkte på en finanspostkonto</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-115">To post a transaction directly to a general ledger account</span></span>
1. <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-116">Vælg ikonet ![Søg efter side eller rapport](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Ikonet Søg efter side eller rapport"), angiv **Finanskladder**, og vælg derefter det relaterede link.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-116">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **General Journals**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>
2. <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-117">Åbn det relevante finanskladdenavn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-117">Open the relevant general journal batch.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-118">Du kan finde flere oplysninger under [Arbejde med finanskladder](ui-work-general-journals.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-118">For more information, see [Working with General Journals](ui-work-general-journals.md).</span></span>
3. <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-119">Udfyld felterne efter behov på en ny kladdelinje.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-119">On a new journal line, fill in the fields as necessary.</span></span> [!INCLUDE[tooltip-inline-tip](includes/tooltip-inline-tip_md.md)]    
4. <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-120">Gentag trin 3 for alle separate transaktioner, du vil bogføre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-120">Repeat step 3 for all the separate transactions that you want to post.</span></span>

    > [!TIP]  
    > <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-121">Hvis du vil angive flere transaktionslinjer over én modkontolinje, f.eks. for én bankkonto, skal du markere afkrydsningsfeltet **Foreslå modkontobeløb** på linjen for kørslen i vinduet **Finanskladdenavne**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-121">If you want to enter multiple transaction lines above one balance-account line, for example, for one bank account, then select the **Suggest Balancing Amount** check box on the line for your batch in the **General Journal Batches** window.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-122">Feltet **Beløb** på modkontolinjen er forudfyldt automatisk med den værdi, der skal bruges til at afstemme posteringerne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-122">Then the **Amount** field on the balance-account line is automatically prefilled with the value that is required to balance the transactions.</span></span>
5. <span data-ttu-id="24fe2-123">Vælg handlingen **Bogfør** for at registrere transaktionerne på de angivne konti i Finans.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="24fe2-123">Choose the **Post** action to record the transactions on the specified G/L accounts.</span></span>

# <a name="sell-items-assembled-to-order"></a><span data-ttu-id="14adf-104">Sælge varer, der er monteret til ordre</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-104">Sell Items Assembled to Order</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="14adf-105">Hvis feltet **Montagepolitik** på varekortet til et montageelement er **Montage efter ordre**, kan varen ikke forventes at være på lager og skal samles specifikt til en salgsordre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-105">If the **Assembly Policy** field on the item card of an assembly item is **Assemble-to-Order**, then the item is not expected to be in inventory, and it must be assembled specifically to a sales order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-106">Når du indtaster varen på en salgsordrelinje, bliver der automatisk oprettet en montageordre, hvorefter den knyttes til salgsordren.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-106">When you enter the item on a sales order line, then an assembly order is automatically created and linked to the sales order.</span></span>  

> [!NOTE]  
>  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-107">Hvis nogle montage efter ordre-elementer allerede findes i lageret, kan de fratrække mængden fra montageordre og reservere den fra lageret.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-107">If some assemble-to-order items are already in inventory, then you can deduct that quantity from the assembly order and reserve it from inventory.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-108">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Sælge lagervarer i montage til ordre-flows](assembly-how-to-sell-assemble-to-order-items-and-inventory-items-together.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-108">For more information, see [Sell Inventory Items in Assemble-to-Order Flows](assembly-how-to-sell-assemble-to-order-items-and-inventory-items-together.md).</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="14adf-109">I denne procedure behandler du salg af et element, der skal samles i henhold til specifikationer, der er anmodet af debitor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-109">In this procedure, you process the sale of an item that will be assembled according to specifications that are requested by the customer.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-110">Fremgangsmåden omfatter start af salgsordrelinje, tilpasning af montageelement ved at redigere dens komponenter og ressourcer, kontrol af tilgængelighed til at fastsætte en leveringsdato og frigive salgsordre skal samles og leveres med det samme.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-110">The steps include initiating the sales order line, customizing the assembly item by editing its components and resources, checking availability to establish a delivery date, and releasing the sales order to be assembled and immediately shipped.</span></span>  

> [!NOTE]  
>  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-111">Følgende procedure omfatter ikke standardsalgsordretrin før det trin, hvor du indtaster montage efter ordre-elementer på en salgsordrelinje.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-111">The following procedure does not include the standard sales order steps before the step where you enter the assemble-to-order item on a sales order line.</span></span>  

## <a name="to-sell-an-item-that-is-assembled-to-order"></a><span data-ttu-id="14adf-112">Sådan sælger du en vare, der er samlet til ordre</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-112">To sell an item that is assembled to order</span></span>  
1.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-113">Vælg ikonet ![Søg efter side eller rapport](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Ikonet Søg efter side eller rapport"), angiv **Salgsordrer**, og vælg derefter det relaterede link.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-113">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Sales Orders**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>  
2.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-114">Oprette en salgsordre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-114">Create a sales order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-115">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Sælge produkter](sales-how-sell-products.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-115">For more information, see [Sell Products](sales-how-sell-products.md).</span></span>  
3.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-116">I feltet **Nummer**</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-116">In the **No.**</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-117">skal du angive en vare, der er indstillet til at samles til ordre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-117">field, enter an item that is set up to be assembled to order.</span></span>  
4.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-118">I feltet **Lokationskode** skal du definere, hvilken lokation varen sælges fra.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-118">In the **Location Code** field, define which location the item will be sold from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-119">Montageprocessen vil forekomme på denne placering.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-119">The assembly process will occur at that location.</span></span>  
5.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-120">I feltet **Antal** skal du angive, hvor mange enheder, der skal sælges.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-120">In the **Quantity** field, enter how many units to sell.</span></span>  

    > [!NOTE]  
    >  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-121">Hvis en eller flere komponenter af antallet af anmodede montageelementer ikke er tilgængelige, åbnes et vindue med en detaljeret tilgængelighedsadvarsel.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-121">If one or more components of the requested assembly item quantity are not available, then a detailed availability warning window opens.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-122">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i Montagedisponering</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-122">For more information, see Assembly Availability.</span></span>  

    <span data-ttu-id="14adf-123">Der oprettes nu ikke automatisk en montageordre med tilknytning til salgsordrelinjen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-123">An assembly order is now automatically created and linked to the sales order line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-124">Forfaldsdato for denne montageordre er synkroniseret med afsendelsesdato for salgsordrelinjen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-124">The due date of this assembly order is synchronized with the shipment date of the sales order line.</span></span>  

    <span data-ttu-id="14adf-125">Antal, der skal sælges, kopieres til feltet **Antal til montage efter ordre**, som angiver, at vareopsætningen forventer, at hele mængden på salgslinjen skal samles til ordren.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-125">The quantity to sell is copied to the **Qty. to Assemble to Order** field, which indicates that the item setup expects the full quantity on the sales line to be assembled to the order.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-126">Du kan reducere antallet til montage efter ordre, hvis du f.eks ved, at nogle elementer allerede er tilgængelige.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-126">You can decrease the quantity to assemble to order, such as if you know that some items are already available.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-127">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Sælge lagervarer i montage til ordre-flows](assembly-how-to-sell-inventory-items-in-assemble-to-order-flows.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-127">For more information, see [Sell Inventory Items in Assemble-to-Order Flows](assembly-how-to-sell-inventory-items-in-assemble-to-order-flows.md).</span></span>  

6.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-128">For at vise, at debitor ønsker yderligere et element i en pakke, skal du på oversigtspanelet **Linjer** vælge handlingen **Linje**, vælge handlingen **Montage til ordre** og derefter vælge handlingen **Montage til ordre-linjer** for at få vist og ændret standardmontagekomponenterne.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-128">To reflect that the customer wants an additional item in a kit, on the **Lines** FastTab, choose the **Line** action, choose the **Assemble to Order** action, and then choose the **Assemble-to-Order Lines** action to view and change the standard assembly components.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-129">Du kan også vælge feltet **Antal til montage efter ordre**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-129">Alternatively, choose the **Qty. to Assemble to Order** field.</span></span>  
7.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-130">I vinduet **Montage efter ordre-linjer** oprettes en ny linje af typen **Element** for det ønskede ekstra pakkeindhold.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-130">In the **Assemble-to-Order Lines** window, create a new line of type **Item** for the requested additional kit content.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-131">Linjen repræsenterer en ekstra montagekomponent.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-131">The line represents an additional assembly component.</span></span>  

    <span data-ttu-id="14adf-132">Du kan også tilpasse rækkefølgen ved at øge antallet af ét standardelement i pakken.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-132">You could also customize the order by increasing the quantity of one standard item in the kit.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-133">Du kan gøre dette ved at forøge værdien i feltet **Mængde pr.** på den pågældende montageordrelinje.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-133">You can do this by increasing the value in the **Quantity Per** field on the specific assembly order line.</span></span>  

    > [!NOTE]  
    >  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-134">Vinduet **Montage efter ordre-linjer** indeholder kun de vigtigste felter, som en sælger forventes at bruge til at tilpasse komponentoversigten, tilføje varesporingsnumre eller løse problemer med komponentdisponibilitet.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-134">The **Assemble-to-Order Lines** window only contains the basic fields that a salesperson is expected to use to customize the component list, add item tracking numbers, or to solve component availability issues.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-135">Hvis du vil have vist flere montageordreoplysninger, f.eks. montageordrens startdato, skal du vælge handlingen **Vis dokumenter**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-135">To see more assembly order information, such as the assembly order starting date, choose the **Show Documents** action.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-136">Dette åbner en komplet visning af den montageordre, der er tilknyttet salgsordrelinjen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-136">This opens a full view of the assembly order that is linked to the sales order line.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-137">Du kan ikke ændre indholdet af de fleste felter i montageordrehovedet, og du kan ikke bogføre montageafgange, fordi du skal benytte leverancebogføringen fra salgsordrelinjen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-137">You cannot change the contents of most fields on the assembly order header, and you cannot post assembly output from it because you must use shipment posting of the sales order line.</span></span>  
    >   
    >  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-138">På sidehovedet i sammenkædede montageordrer, er det kun feltet **Startdato**, der kan ændres for at få montagearbejdere til at angive en dato, der ligger før forfaldsdato, når de starter processen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-138">On the header of linked assembly orders, only the **Starting Date** field can be changed to enable assembly workers to specify a date that is earlier than the due date when they will start the process.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-139">Alle felter på linjerne i den tilknyttede montageordre kan ændres, så lagermedarbejdere kan indtaste tallene for forbrug under processen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-139">All fields on the lines of the linked assembly order can be changed so that warehouse workers can enter consumption figures during the process.</span></span>  

8.  <span data-ttu-id="14adf-140">Gennemse eller reager på problemer med tilgængeligheden af komponenter.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-140">Review or react to component availability issues.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-141">Vælg f.eks. en tilgængelig erstatningsvare, eller fastsæt en senere forfaldsdato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-141">For example, select an available substitute item or establish a later due date.</span></span>  
9. <span data-ttu-id="14adf-142">Luk vinduet **Montage efter ordre-linjer**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-142">Close the **Assemble-to-Order Lines** window.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-143">Den tilknyttede montageordre er nu klar til at begynde samling af de tilpassede elementer efter forfaldsdato.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-143">The linked assembly order is now ready to start to assemble the customized items by the due date.</span></span>  
10. <span data-ttu-id="14adf-144">På salgsordren skal du vælge handlingen **Frigiv** for at advisere montageafdelingen om, at montageprocessen kan startes.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-144">On the sales order, choose the **Release** action to notify the assembly department that the assembly process can start.</span></span>  
11. <span data-ttu-id="14adf-145">I samleafdelingen, skal du udføre trinnene med samling af de elementer, der sælges i denne procedure.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-145">In the assembly department, perform the steps of assembling the items that are sold in this procedure.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="14adf-146">Du kan finde flere oplysninger i [Montere varer](assembly-how-to-assemble-items.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="14adf-146">For more information, see [Assemble Items](assembly-how-to-assemble-items.md).</span></span>
